Agriculture Hub

Origin

Our Story

Agriculture Hub was founded by Naresh Dasari with the vision of creating a platform that bridges the gap between agriculture students, professionals, startups, universities, and the industry. Soon after, Sreekanth Gundelli joined the mission as Co-Founder, sharing the same passion for empowering the agricultural community.

As agriculture graduates, we experienced the challenges faced by students in finding quality internships, career guidance, networking opportunities, and industry exposure. While opportunities existed, they were scattered and difficult to access.

To solve this problem, Agriculture Hub was established as a community-driven platform where students and professionals can discover opportunities, learn from industry experts, connect with organizations, and build successful careers.

Today, Agriculture Hub is growing into a trusted ecosystem that connects thousands of agriculture students and professionals through LinkedIn, Instagram, WhatsApp communities, webinars, workshops, and digital initiatives.

"We believe that the future of agriculture lies in knowledge sharing, innovation, collaboration, and empowering the next generation of agricultural leaders."

Global Opportunities

Our goal is to bridge the gap between academia and industry, helping young talent become future innovators and leaders in agriculture.
