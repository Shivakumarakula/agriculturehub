Introduction

Welcome to Agriculture Hub. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at privacy@agriculturehub.com.

When you visit our website, register an account, and use our services (such as applying for jobs, attending webinars, or accessing mentorship), you trust us with your personal information. We take this responsibility very seriously.

2

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the Agriculture Hub platform, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, or otherwise contact us.

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number, and account passwords.
  • Professional Information: Education details, university name, resumes, portfolios, and career interests (necessary for job placements and mentorship).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, pages visited, and time spent.
3

How We Use Your Information

We use personal information collected via our platform for a variety of business purposes described below:

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process.
  • To match you with potential employers, internships, and agricultural startups.
  • To send administrative information to you, such as updates to terms, conditions, and policies.
  • To notify you about upcoming webinars, workshops, and industry networking events.
  • To deliver targeted career guidance and mentorship opportunities.
4

Sharing of Information

We strictly do not sell your personal data to third-party advertising networks. We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations.

We may share your professional profile (with your permission) with partnered AgriTech companies, universities, and recruiters solely for the purpose of career advancement and networking.

5

Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, despite our safeguards and efforts to secure your information, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to request access and obtain a copy of your personal information.
  • The right to request rectification or erasure of your data.
  • The right to restrict the processing of your personal information.
  • The right to opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
7

Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies (like web beacons and pixels) to access or store information. Specific information about how we use such technologies and how you can refuse certain cookies is set out in our Cookie Policy.
